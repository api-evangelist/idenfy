Choosing KYC onboarding software is a risk management decision - and getting it wrong shows up later in manual workarounds, abandoned onboarding flows, and compliance gaps that only surface during an audit. This blog post cuts through the vendor noise to focus on what actually matters: document verification accuracy, multi-jurisdictional compliance coverage, conversion rates, and the integration and support questions most teams don't think to ask until it's too late.
